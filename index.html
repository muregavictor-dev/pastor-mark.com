About Pastor Mark

Pastor Mark Mutethia Mfalme is the CEO of Fragrance Network, a psychologist specializing in therapy, counseling, and consultancy, and a dedicated music coach and trainer.
He holds a BSc in Psychology from Egerton University and serves as a bishop.

Born in Meru, Kenya, and currently residing in Meru,
he is passionate about mentoring and uplifting others through his spiritual and professional expertise.

He studied at Chuka Boys High School before pursuing his higher education at Egerton University.

With a strong online presence, Pastor Mark has a following of over 5.5K people and frequently shares motivational and spiritual insights on social media.

Life Events & Inspirations

Pastor Mark's passion for ministry was significantly shaped by Apostle Junior Leisido, who mentored him and instilled a deep commitment to crusades and spiritual leadership.

He firmly believes that conflicts can be transformed into opportunities for growth, advocating for a biblical approach to resolution through his teachings.

Publications

The Best Way with Conflicts

Pastor Mark Mutethia Mfalme's book, The Best Way with Conflicts, provides a biblical approach to navigating conflicts and transforming discord into opportunities for growth. This insightful book is designed to help individuals and communities resolve disputes in a constructive and God-centered way.

If you're looking for guidance on handling conflicts with wisdom and grace, this book is a must-read.
