Who is Pastor Mark?

Pastor Mark Mutethia Mfalme is a man devoted to spiritual growth, healing, and mentorship. As the CEO of Fragrance Network, he has dedicated his life to guiding individuals through the power of faith, psychology, and music.

He is a certified psychologist specializing in therapy, counseling, and consultancy. With a BSc in Psychology from Egerton University, he merges scientific knowledge with spiritual wisdom to help people overcome life's challenges.

A gifted musician and trainer, Pastor Mark believes that music is a divine tool for healing and transformation. His mentorship has impacted many aspiring musicians and worship leaders.

Early Life & Education


Born in Meru, Kenya, and currently residing in Nakuru, Pastor Mark's journey has been one of deep faith and dedication. He studied at Chuka Boys High School before advancing to Egerton University, where he pursued his passion for psychology.

His ministry is deeply rooted in personal experiences, mentorship, and a divine calling that has shaped his leadership and service.

Ministry & Impact


Pastor Mark is more than a spiritual leader; he is a mentor and a visionary. His ministry focuses on:

  • Providing spiritual guidance to individuals and communities
  • Offering counseling and therapy to those in need
  • Empowering musicians and worship leaders through music coaching
  • Teaching biblical conflict resolution for personal and communal growth

His passion for ministry was significantly shaped by Apostle Junior Leisido, who mentored him and instilled a deep commitment to serving in crusades and spiritual leadership.
